This means YOU get to download NASA data and use the same tools we astronomers use to create the most beautiful image of another celestial object.

This year's subject is M87, the galaxy hosting that nearby supermonster black hole of ours directly imaged by the Event Horizon Telescope in 2019. There's good reason M87 was the first target for the EHT. It's also been observed by the Chandra X-ray Telescope (which funds me), Hubble, and virtually every big telescope out there, all across the electromagnetic spectrum.

Learn more and watch some stunning animations in this NASA video I got to be part of!
